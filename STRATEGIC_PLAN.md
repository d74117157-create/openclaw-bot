## 🔍 SEO + KEYWORD DOMINATION

### YouTube SEO Strategy
1. **Keyword Research:** TubeBuddy + VidIQ + Google Trends
2. **Title Formula:** "[Number] + [Adjective] + [Keyword] + [Year]"
   - "7 AI Tools That Make $1K/Day (2026)"
   - "How I Built a Bot Army in 24 Hours (Step-by-Step)"
3. **Description:** Hook + timestamps + affiliate links + hashtags
4. **Tags:** 15 tags, mix of broad and long-tail
5. **Thumbnail:** Face + emotion + text overlay + contrasting colors

### TikTok SEO Strategy
1. **Hook in 0-3 seconds:** "Stop scrolling if you want to make money"
2. **Hashtags:** 3-5 max, mix of trending + niche
3. **Sounds:** Use trending sounds within 24 hours
4. **Text overlay:** Key points highlighted
5. **CTA:** "Link in bio" or "Comment 'EMPIRE' for the free guide"

### Google SEO Strategy
1. **Blog posts** (Wix blog): Target long-tail keywords
2. **YouTube videos** rank on Google
3. **Backlinks:** Guest posts, Reddit, Quora
4. **Schema markup:** Rich snippets for products
